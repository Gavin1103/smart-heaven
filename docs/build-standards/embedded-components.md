## Jumper Wires (Dupont Wires)
![wires](/docs/assets/Embedded-components%20assets/wires.webp)

Used to connect components on a breadboard or to the ESP32.

•	Male to Male → both sides have a pin

•	Male to Female → one side has a pin, the other side has a hole

•	Female to Female → both sides have holes
They are used to connect power and signals.

•	Red = power (for example 3.3V or 5V)

•	Black = GND (0 volts)

•	Other colors = signal wires

# Buzzers
## Active Buzzer and passive buzzer
![buzzer](/docs/assets/Embedded-components%20assets/Passive%20Buzzer.jpg)

Active buzzer
Has a built-in oscillator.
Makes sound when you connect DC power.
(Usually has a sticker on it.)
Pasive buzzer
No built-in oscillator.
Needs a PWM signal to make sound.
(No sticker.)
